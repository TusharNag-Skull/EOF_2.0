About Evenly Odd Films

Passionate filmmakers dedicated to creating authentic, impactful visual stories that connect with audiences.

LOGO KI KAHAANI

The logo of Evenly Odd Films beautifully blends the harmony of opposites - sunrise and night; symbolizing the balance between creativity and imagination. The rising sun and birds evoke growth and boundless storytelling, while the moonlit sky reflects dreams, cinema, and aspiration. The western-style typography adds a bold, cinematic character, representing timeless storytelling with a distinctive edge.

Evenly Odd Films is a film production house based in Ahmedabad which was launched in June 2023. The meaning behind the name 'Evenly Odd Films' is; Crafting something simple stories but with a unique touch which will CREATE THE DIFFERENCE. The name suggests that we create unique films which have stunning visuals and emotional storylines that leave a lasting impression.

We believe in taking challenges and risks with our films because that gives the best results and a new cinema is born. The best work comes from passion and collaboration and our team consists of passionate people who believes in creating a cinematic experience film.

About the Founder:

Hardik Soni, founder of Evenly Odd Films, is a young passionate filmmaker who believes in creating unique set of films which will contribute to the society. He is a Director, Actor, Editor, Writer, and Producer. He has 5+ years of experience in editing, 3+ years of experience in acting, 2+ years of experience in film direction, and 1+ Years of experience in writing.

He is also working with the KYA HAAL CHAAL BY APOORV JHA podcast. He has started in directorial journey with the foundation of Evenly Odd Films.

He is going to release his debut feature film in next year.
